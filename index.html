About Me

I am a college sophomore at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, studying electrical engineering. I plan to use the creativity and leadership skills I have to solve complicated problems and work towards making my community a better place. I am a problem-solver, enjoy learning, and viewing a problem from multiple perspectives.

"Your personal life, your professional life, and your creative life are all intertwined. I went through a few very difficult years where I felt like a failure. But it was actually really important for me to go through that. Struggle, for me, is the most inspirational thing in the world at the end of the day - as long as you treat it that way."
- Skylar Grey
